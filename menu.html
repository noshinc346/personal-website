Introduction

meNu is a social media app designed to bring food enthusiasts together by allowing them to share and discover recipes. This platform enables users to post pictures of their dishes, share detailed recipes, and engage with others through comments. Users can also create personalized profiles, showcasing their culinary creations in a dedicated list.

Features

Public Feed

  • Users can share images of their dishes along with detailed recipes.
  • Other users can comment on these posts, fostering a community of food lovers.

Profile Page

  • Each user has a customizable profile where they can personalize their information.
  • A list of all the recipes a user has posted will be displayed on their profile, making it easy to showcase their culinary skills.

Following

  • Users can follow each other to keep up with their favorite recipe creators and see their latest posts on their feed.

Search Bar

  • A powerful search feature allows users to find specific recipes by keywords, ingredients, or dish names in public posts.

User Story

Amanda's Culinary Adventure - Busy working professional and aspiring chef
She wants to: Easily share her quick and healthy recipes with others, discover new meal ideas, and connect with fellow food enthusiasts
So that she can: Simplify her cooking routine, gain inspiration for creative dishes, and engage with a supportive community passionate about cooking

Development Status

Currently, meNu is in the planning and initial development stages. The core features and architecture have been outlined, and the next steps involve building the frontend and backend components, integrating the database, and implementing the follow and search functionalities.

Conclusion

meNu aims to create a vibrant community where food enthusiasts can share their passion for cooking, discover new recipes, and connect with like-minded individuals. By leveraging modern web technologies like React and Express Mongoose, we plan to deliver a seamless and enjoyable user experience. This project showcases my ability to design and develop a full-stack application, emphasizing user interaction and community building.
